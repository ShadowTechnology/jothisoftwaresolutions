FAQ

Common Questions About Meta Ads

What is the minimum budget for Facebook Ads in Ambur?

We recommend starting with a minimum ad spend of ₹10,000–₹30,000 per month for meaningful results. This allows enough budget to test different audiences and creatives. However, the ideal budget depends on your industry and goals. Jothi Software Solutions helps you plan a budget that makes sense for your business size and growth targets.

Can Meta Ads target customers specifically in Ambur and Tamil Nadu?

Yes. Meta Ads allow precise geographic targeting — you can target users within a specific radius of Ambur, specific cities in Tamil Nadu, or even pin-drop locations. We use this geo-targeting capability to ensure your ads reach the local audience most relevant to your business, minimizing wasted spend on irrelevant locations.

What types of Meta Ad campaigns do you run?

We manage all Meta Ad campaign types: Brand Awareness, Traffic, Engagement, Lead Generation (with instant forms), Conversions, and Catalogue Sales. For most local businesses in Ambur, we recommend starting with Lead Generation campaigns that capture contact details directly within Facebook/Instagram without requiring users to visit your website.

How do you measure the success of Meta Ads?

We track key metrics including cost-per-lead, cost-per-click, click-through rate, conversion rate, and return on ad spend (ROAS). We install the Meta Pixel on your website to track website actions and attribute them to specific ads. Monthly reports clearly show how many leads and sales your ad investment generated.
